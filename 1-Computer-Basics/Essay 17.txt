Content: The words, images, videos, data and everything people create with computer programs and equipment, to display on webpages and sites so that other users can view/hear what other users have created.
SEO:  Search Engine Optimization, the way a search engine will rank websites when a search term is inputted to into it, and how those websites can be ideally made to come at the top of such search results to make the best possible search experience.
Social bookmarking: Telling someone else about some cool and nifty websites. Many social media websites make this easier to share to people, and some sites are dedicated just to this concept.
