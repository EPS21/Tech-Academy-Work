Backup - To duplicate data and store it elsewhere, for the express purpose of saving it in the event the original copy gets destroyed.
Cache - Saved data stored in various local areas so when to be recalled again it is quicker to retrieve than where its source was.
Cloud Computing - Running your programs from an external computer rather than your own and still controlling some aspect of it from your own. Will also use some part of cloud storage usually.
Cookie - Files saved to your computer from websites, so that next time you visit it it can remember your preferences and settings.
Sharepoint - Microsoft's cloud software that business can use to access their documents, and coordinate all their users easier.
Source - The origin of something, or in business, where they locate goods and services to use for their business.
Word Processing - The program you typed all your essays on while you were in school. And everyone uses it to make basic text documents.
